About Us

The Fake News Detector is an intelligent web application designed to identify fake and misleading news. Our goal is to help people verify information before believing or sharing it on social media.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safer digital world by reducing the spread of misinformation. We encourage users to verify news from trusted sources and promote responsible online behavior.

Our Vision

We aim to build a society where people can easily distinguish between real and fake news using modern technology and artificial intelligence.

  • Supports awareness against misinformation.
